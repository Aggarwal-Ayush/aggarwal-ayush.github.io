Research Interest

I'm a PhD student in Biological Sciences working with Dr Vivek T. Natarajan at CSIR - Institute of Genomics and Integrative Biology, Delhi, India.
I work in the field of skin pigmentation focusing on melanocyte biology. My current research project involves deciphering the mechanism and the biological significance behind melanocyte cell state/lineage transitions using a mix of informatics and experimental approaches.

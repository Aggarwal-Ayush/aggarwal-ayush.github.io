Ayush Aggarwal
PhD Student
CSIR-IGIB

PhD student in Biological Sciences working with Dr Vivek T. Natarajan at CSIR - IGIB, Delhi, India in the field of skin pigmentation and focusing on melanocyte biology. My PhD project involves understanding the melanocyte cell state/lineage transitions using a mix of computational and experimental approaches. Learn More.

Skilled in computational as well as experimental biology techniques

Want to see how your favourite gene behaves across multiple melanoycte developmetal and functional states? Check out MelDat a shiny web application I developed to quickly explore melanocyte related (bulk and single-cell) datasets. You can even visualize your own RNA seq or microarray datasets and create publication quality figures.

Ayush Aggarwal

I'm a PhD student in Biological Sciences at CSIR - Institute of Genomics and Integrative Biology, Delhi, India. I work in the field of skin pigmentation focusing on melanocyte biology. My current research project involves deciphering the mechanism and the biological significance behind melanocyte cell state transitions in normal physiology.

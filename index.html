Ayush Aggarwal
PhD Student
CSIR-IGIB

I'm a PhD student in Biological Sciences working with Dr Vivek T. Natarajan at CSIR - Institute of Genomics and Integrative Biology, Delhi, India.
I work in the field of skin pigmentation focusing on melanocyte biology. My current research project involves deciphering the mechanism and the biological significance behind melanocyte cell state/lineage transitions using a mix of informatics and experimental approaches. Learn More

Want to see how your favourite gene behaves across multiple melanoycte developmetal and functional states? Check out MelDat a shiny web application I developed to quickly explore melanocyte biology related (bulk and single-cell) datasets. You can even visualize your own RNA seq or microarray datasets and create publication quality figures.
